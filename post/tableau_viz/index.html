C YPS are measured to provide an estimation of the contraceptive coverage of a population. CYPS are interpreted more as an indication of the intensity of the ESHE program activity, as opposed to a measure of the use of contraceptives.

FAQs

Please find below the answers to some of the frequently asked questions.

  • What are CYPs?


    CYP stand for couple year protection. It is the estimated protection provided by contraceptive methods during a one-year period, based upon the volume of all contraceptives sold or distributed free of charge to clients during that period.



  • How are CYPs calculated?


    The CYP is calculated by multiplying the quantity of each method distributed to clients by a conversion factor, to yield an estimate of the duration of contraceptive protection provided per unit of that method.
